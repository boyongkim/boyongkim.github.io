Curriculum Vitae

Boyong Kim has an illustrious career in the arts, having exhibited in galleries worldwide. This page provides a comprehensive overview of her education, exhibitions, and awards.

VITA

  • 1971 born in Seoul
  • 1990 Commencement of Fine Arts at the Seoul Woman's University (Seoul)
  • 1995 Graduation in Fine Arts
  • 1996 Foundation of the private school for painting "Mire Hyeang" in Seoul
  • 1997 Move to Germany
  • 1998 Commencement of history of art at the "Technische Universität Berlin"
  • since 2002 freelance artist
  • since 2004 living and working in Nuremberg
